CITY CHILDREN SCHOOL was established in Gwalior in the year 2006. Affiliated to the State Board, Madhya Pradesh, it has been running successfully over the years and has grown since inception from KG to High School. Its unique curriculum, zest, zeal and an onward driving force have made it possible to attain the highest levels of excellence.

We do not teach our student’s only sciences and arts, we do not only keep them physically and academically busy with hosts of extra–curricular and co-curricular activities, but we also cater to the need of their soul by moulding them into morally upright citizens of tomorrow who will make their contribution to the advancement of human society…because this is the main purpose for which a child is sent in this world by our loving Creator.
